# 🧷 **5. STYLE INTEGRITY RULES (IMPORTANT)**

To keep all future covers consistent:

### DO:

* Always use **black** as the background
* Always keep illustrations **abstract**
* Always use **one accent color only**
* Keep line weights **monoline**
* Maintain **balance**: center focus + wide margins
* Add **metadata** (report IDs, grid refs, specimen codes) in Lexend

### DO NOT:

* No photos
* No 3D rendering
* No realistic objects (no paper, no gears, no real charts)
* No gradients except soft glows
* No character silhouettes or figurative content
